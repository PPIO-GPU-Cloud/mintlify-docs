Welcome to InfrAI, your one-stop solution for GPU compute power in the cloud. ## Payment Methods
Currently, we accept payments exclusively through:
- **Credit Card**: A widely-used and convenient option, allowing for quick and secure transactions. Please ensure that your card has sufficient funds to cover your compute requirements.
- **Business Invoicing**: For transactions, we provide business invoices. This is ideal for enterprises and organizations looking to manage their compute expenses efficiently.
